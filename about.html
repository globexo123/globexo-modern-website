About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Globexo Naturals is a Pakistani-based company specializing in premium quality Himalayan pink salt, sourced directly from the mines in Khewra, Pakistan.

We are committed to providing the purest form of Himalayan salt that contains all 84+ essential minerals found in the human body.

Our Mission

To promote health and wellness through natural mineral-rich products while maintaining sustainable mining practices.

Our Vision

To become a global leader in Himalayan salt products while preserving the natural environment.
